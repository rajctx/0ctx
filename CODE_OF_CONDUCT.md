# Code of Conduct

0ctx is committed to a respectful, constructive, and technically rigorous
community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, direct, and professional.
- Assume good intent, but challenge ideas with evidence.
- Keep discussions focused on the code, docs, design, and user impact.
- Share actionable feedback and clear reproduction steps when reporting bugs.
- Respect privacy, security boundaries, and embargoed disclosures.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination based on identity or protected characteristics.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or repeated bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information, secrets, or security-sensitive details without permission.
- Abusive or hostile communication in issues, pull requests, discussions, or chat.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests,
documentation, and any other project-managed communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of
conduct. They may also suspend or ban contributors from participating in the
project when needed to protect the community and the repository.

## Reporting

For general conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the repository
support path documented in [SUPPORT.md](./SUPPORT.md).

For security-sensitive concerns, follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) instead of
posting publicly.
